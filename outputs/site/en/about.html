Our Story

About Mehrpouyan Sabz Andisheh NGO (MSANGO)

From a Dream to Reality — a journey that began with a volunteer-led group in 2016 and was officially registered in September 2017.

Living in a society with limited social action drew us toward adult citizenship education and community development — and toward the poor and marginal districts of our city, Isfahan. To help establish a "sustainable community," we relied on existing social capital and people's active collaboration to take steps, however small, to empower and develop deprived human communities.

Our shared motivation, commitment, and ideas created a strong sense of belonging, which led to the formation of an active community in 2016 — later officially registered as Mehrpouyan Sabz Andisheh NGO (MSANGO) in 2017.

Mission

Supporting vulnerable social groups

Our mission is to actively support and participate in growing the capacities of vulnerable social groups and underserved neighborhoods.

Vision

Human development, community-based

Actualizing the process of human and local development in marginal urban and rural areas through a community-based development approach.

Our Values

Ten principles that guide our work

1

Belief in human dignity, agency, and equal access to opportunity for women and children, especially the disadvantaged.

2

Working toward sustainable development and peace.

3

Striving to reduce poverty, avoid violence, and realize social justice.

4

Transparency and responsiveness in civil institutions, and openness to critique and dialogue.

5

Cultivating a participatory, community-based approach in thought and practice.

6

Belief in the role of education, counseling, and skills development for wellbeing and access to work.

7

Supporting disadvantaged women and children in identifying and growing their capabilities.

8

Using local capacities free of ethnic, religious, or gender-based discrimination.

9

Drawing on theory, research, and practical models — both global and local — to improve our work.

10

Mutual collaboration with civil, private, and government institutions toward our shared goals.

Target Community

Disadvantaged mothers and children in urban and rural marginal areas who, due to low income and limited access to resources, have fewer opportunities for education and for building social, communication, and professional skills. MSANGO is currently active in Arzanan, Zeinabieh St., District 14, Isfahan.

Our Approach

A participatory approach to education, activities, and governance; a development-oriented, peaceful outlook; poverty reduction and social justice through community-based development; and growing awareness, cooperation, and a sense of belonging among all stakeholders.

Our Goals

What we're working toward

  • ▸ Educate and empower disadvantaged families in urban and rural marginal areas.
  • ▸ Improve life skills and capabilities of women and children.
  • ▸ Strengthen and promote voluntary, cooperative-based activities.
  • ▸ Train and organize social activists, facilitators, and outreach workers.
  • ▸ Hold seminars and workshops, and produce educational content related to our goals.
Mehrpouyan Sabz Andisheh

A non-profit, non-political, community-based NGO active since 2016 in the Arzanan neighborhood of Isfahan, working on education, empowerment and local development.
